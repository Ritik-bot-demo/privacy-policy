Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ritik Bot ("we", "our", or "the application") collects, uses, and protects information when using the Meta Platform, including the Instagram API and related services.

1. Purpose of the Application

Ritik Bot is an automation application created to help manage an Instagram Business account. The application may:

  • Automatically respond to Instagram Direct Messages (DMs).
  • Send keyword-triggered automated replies.
  • Reply to comments on content owned by the connected Instagram account.
  • Send direct messages based on interactions with the account's content.

2. Information We Access

With the permission of the account owner and through Meta's APIs, the application may access:

  • Instagram Business account information.
  • Messages sent to the connected Instagram account.
  • Comments posted on the connected account's content.
  • Basic public profile information necessary to operate automation features.

3. How We Use Information

The information accessed through the Meta Platform is used only to:

  • Respond to direct messages.
  • Detect keywords and send automated responses.
  • Reply to comments.
  • Provide and improve automation functionality.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the application.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect information processed by the application. Access tokens and credentials are used solely for authenticating requests with Meta's services.

5. Third-Party Services

This application uses services provided by Meta Platforms, Inc., including the Instagram Graph API and other Meta Developer Platform services. Use of those services is also governed by Meta's own Terms and Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade user information. Information may only be processed as necessary to provide the requested automation features or where required by law.

7. Data Retention

Information accessed through the Meta Platform is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the application's functionality or comply with legal obligations.
