Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: 13 June 2026

Ritik Bot respects the privacy of its users and provides a method to request deletion of any data associated with the application.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to request deletion of any information associated with your use of this application, please send an email to:

Email: ritik2telegram@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your Instagram username.
  • Your Facebook account email (if applicable).
  • The subject line: Data Deletion Request.

Processing Your Request

Once we receive a valid request, we will review and process it within a reasonable time and will make commercially reasonable efforts to remove any applicable data associated with the application, unless retention is required by law.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding data deletion, please contact:

Email: ritik2telegram@gmail.com
